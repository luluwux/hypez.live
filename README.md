# <p align="center">Hypez.live — Discord Server Discovery Platform</p>
Hypez is the easiest way to discover, grow, and promote your Discord server.

Browse thousands of servers by category, vote for your favorites, and hype the communities you love. If you own a server, add it to Hypez — our Discord bot handles the rest.

Our ranking system goes beyond simple vote counts. Community activity, voice channel usage, and hype scores combine to surface the most active servers.

Hypez is currently in beta — getting better every day.
